About

Women in Power Summit 2021 (WIPS) is a virtual global event organised by IEEE Student Branch College of Engineering Karunagappally in association with Power and Energy Society, Women in Engineering affinity group and Signal Processing Society. This event aims to inspire, educate, and empower students by conducting online workshops and session related to women in power and signal processing.

WIPS 2021 will be held from 26th to 28th November 2021

Students can now visit the Events tab to know more about the sessions and workshops.

Detailed guidelines of the event will be published on 25th November 2021.
