Taking into account the various energy requirement of a building, how can you efficiently power a residential building using renewable energy?

The need for renewable energy projects is increasing at an alarming rate. Ranging from environmental to socio-economic and political advantages, they hold the potential to serve as the most efficient solution to meet the future energy requirements of the world. Combatting climate changes, improvement in public health, stable energy prices, energy security, development of local industries and increasing job opportunities are some of the added advantages it offers.

Although there has been much progress in the use of renewable energy and energy efficiency, with an expanding energy access across the globe, the world is not on track to meet its international climate goals. There are many potential customers who want to board on renewable energy to green their operations, buildings and lives.
