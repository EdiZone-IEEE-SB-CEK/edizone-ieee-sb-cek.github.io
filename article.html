A study on any industry and its impact on the environment.

Dated from 18th century industrialization has brought significant outbreak from the usuals to an elevation in societal economy and upgrade in the standard of living. In perfecting community life and for an easier approach to living, people are ignoring or maybe not noticing the negative impact industrialization has brought to the world or more precisely to “HUMANS" itself. Starting with the steam engine our journey stays never-ending. The era of technology serves a peaceful simplified life revolving individuals but creating instability in the globe.

Pollution, greenhouse gas emission and global warming are heard more often these days. To mention the financial disparity between the labor and the "owner/investor" is financially destroying the minority community. A careful understanding and analysis can help the concerned to ease the impact of industrialization. We are responsible enough to take in the must-know realities we face indirectly. It is not late to take the necessary steps. Let’s do our part and contribution our little effort.

Topic: A study on any industry and its impact on the environment.
